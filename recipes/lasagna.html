Lasagna

Description:

This is a simple recipe of traditional Lasagne, it tastes of italian tradition, grandma' s perfume The "domenica"'s dish. Creamy and tasty it reminds me the lunch with family and the joy of staying together with loved ones.

Ingredients:

  • Minced Meat (Pork and Beef mix): 500 gr
  • Tomatoes: 1 Kg
  • Soffritto (Onion, Carrot, Garlic, Salt)
  • Butter: 300 gr
  • Milk 1 Lt
  • Wheat: 120 gr.
  • Noce moscata powder: just a pinch
  • Mozzarella: 500 gr
  • Parmigiano reggiano: 300 gr
  • Lasagne 500 gr

Prepare the Bolognese Sauce

Cover the bottom of a pan with olive oil, let it get to temperature then add the soffritto (all ingredients must be finely minced). When the onion will take a golden color add the minced meat and shuffle frequently. Add Salt and pepper. When the meat will be cooked add tomatoes sauce, lower the flames and let it cook for 2/3 hours adding some water if needed

The Besciamella Sauce

Melt the butter in a pan, and add the wheat shuffling energically, boil the milk and add it to the butter and what (roux), keep mixing on the fire and let it take a creamy consistence, in the end add salt and noce moscata.

Build the Lasagna Castle

Cover the pan with butter, put a spoon of bolognese sauce and besciamelle sauce on the bottom cover the bottom with lasagne, add both sauce above lasagne, add on top mozzarella and Parmigiano, add another layer of lasagne and repeat all the procedure till you will have a castle of lasagne. Take it and put it in the owen for 45 mins at 180 celsius degrees. Enjoy!!!
