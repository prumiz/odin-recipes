Potatoes Salad

Description:

TThe best potato salads are a balance of flavors and textures, where creamy, tangy, sweet, salty, tender, and crunchy meet in perfect harmony.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Relish
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Seasonings

How Long to Boil Potatoes for Potato Salad

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain, cool, peel and chop.

The eggs

While potatoes cook, place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil; cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.

Build the Potaotes Salad

Combine the potatoes, eggs, celery, onion, relish, mayonnaise, mustard, garlic salt, celery salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Mix together well and refrigerate until chilled.. Enjoy!!!
